# Conscientiousness (C)

## Technical Definition
Conscientiousness reflects the degree of organization, dependability, and discipline. In language, it is characterized by a focus on goals, precision, and the avoidance of informal or sloppy phrasing.

## Prompt Engineering Guidelines
When evaluating for Conscientiousness, look for:
- **High C:** Use of goal-oriented language. Precise and correct grammar. Methodical and structured presentation of ideas. Focus on duty and achievement.
- **Low C:** Use of informal language, slang, or loose grammar. Digressive or unorganized thought patterns. Focus on immediate impulses rather than long-term goals.

**Evaluation Prompt snippet:**
"Rate the text from 0.0 to 1.0 on Conscientiousness (C). 1.0 is highly disciplined, goal-oriented, and precise. 0.0 is informal, impulsive, and disorganized."

## Scoring Examples
- **1.0 (High):** "To ensure maximum efficiency, we have established a 12-step verification protocol that must be followed without deviation."
- **0.5 (Neutral):** "I've made a list of things to do today so I can make sure I get everything done on time."
- **0.0 (Low):** "Eh, whatever, I'll just kind of figure it out as I go. No need to stress about the details right now."
